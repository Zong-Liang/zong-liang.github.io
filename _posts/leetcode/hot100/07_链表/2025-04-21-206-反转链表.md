**示例 1:**

![img](../../../../assets/img/posts/p206_0.jpg)

```java
输入: head = [1,2,3,4,5]
输出: [5,4,3,2,1]
```

**示例 2:**

![img](../../../../assets/img/posts/p206_1.jpg)

```java
输入: head = [1,2]
输出: [2,1]
```
